Jean Smith

Jean Smith is a founding member of RMSC as well as head judge for the club’s dance challenge for 10 years. “Disco made me do it.”

She started teaching dance in1977 teaching disco line dances. Her back ground includes social ballroom and hustle. Also the first instructor to introduce night club two step to the Denver area in 1993. She was the top west coast female instructor for Carter’s dance school for 13 years, as well as dance director for Social dance arts for 4 years. Many local instructors took their first west coast lesson with her. Jean loves working with beginners giving them an understanding of basic steps, connection, and lead follow.
